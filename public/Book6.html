DAN BROWN

"In "Angels & Demons," Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon is summoned to a Swiss research facility to investigate a murder marked by a mysterious symbol, leading him on a perilous journey. He uncovers a plot by the ancient Illuminati to wreak havoc on the Catholic Church. As the Vatican prepares for a conclave, Langdon races against time to stop the Illuminati's plan, aided by the brilliant scientist Vittoria Vetra. Their quest takes them through the historic streets of Rome, into crypts, cathedrals, and secretive vaults, following a trail of ancient symbols. The tension mounts as they decipher clues and confront dark truths, ultimately facing a showdown with the forces threatening Vatican City. "Angels & Demons" is a gripping international thriller that explores the age-old conflict between science and religion, blending historical mysteries with modern-day intrigue."
"The Lost Symbol" by Dan Brown follows Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon as he is unexpectedly summoned to Washington, D.C. to deliver a lecture. However, the night takes a strange turn when a mysterious object with encoded symbols is found in the Capitol Building. When Langdon's mentor is kidnapped, he must decipher the symbols and follow a trail of Masonic secrets to save him. The novel is a fast-paced thriller, filled with hidden history, arcane symbols, and enigmatic codes, delivering surprises at every turn. It's a gripping tale that showcases Brown's mastery of storytelling and leaves readers on the edge of their seats until the very end."
"The Da Vinci Code" follows Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon as he becomes embroiled in a murder investigation at the Louvre in Paris. Teaming up with French cryptologist Sophie Neveu, they uncover a trail of clues hidden in the works of Leonardo da Vinci, leading to a centuries-old secret guarded by the Priory of Sion. Racing against time and facing off against a mysterious powerbroker linked to Opus Dei, a Vatican-sanctioned sect, Langdon and Neveu must decipher the intricate puzzle to unveil a stunning historical truth before it's lost forever. This lightning-paced thriller blends adventure, intrigue, and wit, delivering surprises at every turn and an unpredictable conclusion."
"In "Deception Point," when a rare object is discovered buried in the Arctic ice, NASA sees it as a triumph with far-reaching implications for U.S. space policy and the upcoming presidential election. White House Intelligence analyst Rachel Sexton is sent to verify the find, accompanied by academic Michael Tolland. However, they uncover evidence of scientific manipulation, putting them in the crosshairs of a deadly conspiracy. As they flee for their lives, they must uncover the truth behind the deception before it's too late. Dan Brown's gripping thriller takes readers from the secretive halls of government to the treacherous Arctic landscape, delivering pulse-pounding suspense and unexpected twists at every turn."
"Harvard symbologist Robert Langdon finds himself immersed in the heart of Italy's mysteries as he delves into Dante's Inferno. Battling a chilling adversary and deciphering ingenious riddles, Langdon navigates through classic art, secret passageways, and futuristic science. Drawing from Dante's epic poem, Langdon races against time to uncover answers and determine whom to trust before the world faces irreversible change."
"Digital Fortress" thrusts readers deep into the secretive world of the National Security Agency (NSA), where a cryptographer, Susan Fletcher, confronts a code so complex that it threatens to cripple U.S. intelligence. As she races against time to unravel the mystery and save the agency, Susan finds herself entangled in a web of secrecy, betrayal, and danger. With a startling twist and a relentless pace, the novel takes readers from the corridors of power in Washington D.C. to the bustling streets of Tokyo and the historic cathedrals of Spain."
